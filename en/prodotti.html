Genuine origin

From manual harvesting in the forest to the bag. Every chestnut is chosen carefully to ensure the integrity of the mountain flavour.

Human contact

No automated cart. We want to talk to you, explain the product, and hand it to you here at the farm or deliver it locally.

Untouched grove

Our centuries-old trees grow without chemical fertilisers, immersed in the biodiversity of the mountain undergrowth.

hand_gesture

Manual harvesting

Every burr is opened by hand. A rigorous selection ensuring only the healthiest and sweetest fruits.

grain

Dried chestnuts

Only 1,000 kg per year. Bureaucratically simpler than flour, perfect for soups, cakes and to keep in the pantry for winter.
