The Forest's Urgency

At the heart of our estate, a unique ecosystem is suffocating. 20 centuries-old chestnut trees, gentle giants that fed generations, are today smothered by acacia and invasive ivy.

These monumental trees need an immediate intervention of clearing, conservative pruning and ground stabilisation. Without care, within the next 3 years, root suffocation and lack of light will mark the end of this legacy.

"The chestnut is not just a tree, it is a living monument that speaks of our resilience."
